Introduction to Javascript covered many of the concepts I had learned last year such as loops, logic statements and functions. Natural Simulations


Building off intro to JS, Natural Simulations delved deeper into object oriented design by using physics principles to simulate natural behaviors such as acceleration, oscillations and controlled randomness. Although quite a challenge, this module made me more comfortable with using objects. It even taught me a thing or two about concepts not exclusive to programming such as noise and vectors.

Project: Paint Splatter

Games and Visualisations


Similarly to Natural Simulations, Games and Visualisations also had me learn more about object-oriented design. I learnt about common concepts used in games such as buttons and scene changes. The projects in this module were quite open ended which allowed for a fun learning experience.
